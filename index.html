nursee makes the case for mobile telemedicine applications as portable, effective health care delivery points.
Empowering nurses with a mobile solution to assist them in the process of triage and patient management, it also enables them to communicate remotely with off-site physicians.
nursee is Mani Nilchiani's thesis project for his MFA degree in Design & Technology at Parsons The New School for Design in New York City. Come see the app in our Thesis Exhibition!
nursee is an evolving project. Follow the code-in-progress on github.
nursee iOS application and this website have been crafted with love at mani.art Studio in beautiful Brooklyn.

nursee takes on the challenge of designing medical user interfaces that accommodate large amounts of sensitive medical data, while maintaining a streamlined workflow to facilitate effective and efficient healthcare practice.
Encapsulating patient data in 10 modules, nursee allows practitioners to:

Easily manage detailed patient personal information.

Keep track of appointments and visits.

Maintain and refer to patient's diagnosis history.

Manage patient's personal medical history.

Keep track of patient's medications.

Manage patient's allergies.

Maintain patient's immunization records.

See patient's lab records.

View patient's medical image records.

Maintain patient's lifestyle choices.
